# Required Images for SEO & PWA

Please create and add the following images to the `/public` folder:

## Favicon & Icons
- `favicon.ico` - 32x32 or 48x48 favicon
- `icon-192.png` - 192x192 PNG icon for PWA
- `icon-512.png` - 512x512 PNG icon for PWA
- `apple-icon.png` - 180x180 PNG for Apple devices
- `safari-pinned-tab.svg` - SVG icon for Safari pinned tabs

## Social Media & SEO
- `og-image.png` - 1200x630 PNG for Open Graph (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.)
- `logo.png` - 512x512 PNG logo for schema markup

## Design Guidelines
- Use IPlyzer brand colors: Blue (#2563eb) and Indigo (#4f46e5)
- Include "IP" or network-related iconography
- Keep designs clean and professional
- Ensure good contrast for visibility

## Quick Creation Options
1. Use Canva or Figma with templates
2. Use online favicon generators
3. Use AI tools like DALL-E or Midjourney
4. Hire a designer on Fiverr/Upwork
